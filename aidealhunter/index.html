02 AI Signals

The 4 Motivation Signals
AI Looks For

Artificial intelligence isn't magic; it's a powerful tool for pattern recognition. AI can analyze vast amounts of data to identify subtle signals of seller motivation that are nearly impossible for humans to spot at scale.

01

Financial Distress

The most common and powerful motivator. Financial hardship often forces a property owner's hand.

Data Points:

Pre-foreclosure notices, mortgage lates, liens, bankruptcy filings, unemployment spikes

02

Life Events

Major life changes frequently trigger the need to sell. These events create urgency.

Data Points:

Probate filings, divorce records, marriage licenses, birth announcements, empty-nester indicators

03

Property Neglect

Visible disrepair or code violations indicate an owner unable or unwilling to maintain.

Data Points:

Code violations, utility shut-offs, tax delinquency, satellite imagery analysis

04

Behavioral Patterns

Past behavior predicts future actions. Timing and market conditions create opportunity.

Data Points:

Length of ownership, previous listing history, non-owner occupant status

03 AI Prompts

7 Battle-Tested Prompts
For Scoring Deals

Use these prompts with any powerful language model to analyze and score your leads. They're designed to extract actionable intelligence.

01

Seller Motivation Triage

Assess the likely level of motivation to sell on a scale of 1-10. Justify your score by identifying the top 3 motivation signals present.

Property Address Owner Name Known Data Points
Output: Motivation Score, Justification
02

Property Condition Assessment

Provide a 'Property Condition Score' from 1-5 (1 = Excellent, 5 = Major Rehab Needed). Describe any visible signs of neglect.

Property Address Tax Data Permit History
Output: Condition Score, Signs of Neglect
03

Equity & Financial Analysis

Estimate the owner's equity position and summarize their likely financial situation. Determine if a cash offer would be highly attractive.

Market Value Purchase History Known Liens
Output: Est. Equity, Cash Offer Fit
04

Neighborhood Market Analysis

Provide a 'Neighborhood Pulse' report including days on market, 6-month appreciation rate, and buyer's vs seller's market status.

Property Address Zip Code
Output: Neighborhood Pulse Report
05

Deal Readiness Score

Synthesize all analyses to generate a single 'Deal Readiness Score' from 1-100 representing likelihood to sell at discount.

All Prior Analyses Property Address
Output: Score (1-100), Breakdown
06

First Contact Angle

Write a brief, empathetic 2-3 sentence script for initial contact. Goal: open a conversation, not make an offer.

Key Motivation Signal Contact Method
Output: Opening Script
07

Objection Pre-Emption

Identify top 2 objections this seller will have. Provide one-sentence responses that acknowledge and reframe.

Primary Motivation Seller Type
Output: Objections & Responses
